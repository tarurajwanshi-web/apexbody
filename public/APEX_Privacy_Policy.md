**APEX PRIVACY POLICY**

*Last Updated: June 22, 2026*

# 1. Introduction

APEX (“APEX,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides an AI-powered fitness and recovery coaching application (the “Services”), including the APEX mobile/web application, the APEX Shield readiness scoring engine, and the APEX Intelligence coaching engine. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices you have.

This Policy applies to anyone who creates an APEX account or otherwise uses the Services. It does not apply to third-party services, websites, or wearable platforms (such as WHOOP, Oura, or Garmin) that you may choose to connect to APEX — those services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

**Health Data Notice. **APEX processes health and fitness-related data, including data that may be considered “consumer health data” under applicable law. Please also see our separate APEX Health Data Policy for details specific to this category of information.

# 2. How We Collect Personal Data

We collect personal data about you from:

- You directly, such as when you create an account, complete onboarding (goal, training days, equipment access, body data), or log meals, recovery, or body measurements;

- Photos you submit, such as meal photos for nutrition estimation and physique/progress photos (where this feature is enabled);

- Manual entries, such as recovery check-ins, sleep hours, body fat percentage, and circumference measurements;

- Connected wearables, if and when you choose to connect a device (e.g. WHOOP, Oura, Garmin, Apple Health) — currently via screenshot upload, with direct OAuth integration planned for a future release;

- Conversations with APEX Intelligence (Engine B), our AI coaching feature, as described in Section 8;

- Automatic technical data, such as device type, app version, and crash/diagnostic logs; and

- Payment data, if and when APEX introduces paid subscriptions, processed by a third-party payment processor (not stored directly by APEX).

# 3. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

- Account data: name, email address, authentication identifiers (via Google/Apple sign-in).

- Profile data: age, sex, height, weight, training goal, training days, equipment access.

- Body composition data: body fat percentage, lean mass, and circumference measurements (waist, hip, arms, thigh), including DEXA/InBody-derived data if you choose to upload it. Each entry is stored as a dated record so you can track change over time.

- Wellness and training data: recovery check-ins, sleep duration, training session logs (sets, reps, RIR), training load, and mood/stress check-ins.

- Nutrition data: meal photos and the resulting AI-estimated food items, portions, and macros (calories, protein, carbs, fat), plus any hydration you log. Stored per-entry with a date, so trends can be calculated over time.

- Photos: meal photos, and physique/progress photos where that feature is enabled.

- AI coaching conversations: messages you send to and receive from APEX Intelligence (Engine B), including any context drawn from your Shield score, training history, and logged data to generate a response.

- Device and usage data: app version, device type, general diagnostic and crash data.

# 4. How We Use Personal Data

We use your personal data to:

- Provide the Services, including calculating your APEX Shield readiness score, generating and adjusting your training plan, and powering APEX Intelligence coaching features;

- Estimate meal composition and macros from photos you submit (see Section 8 for details on how this uses AI);

- Store your logged history (meals, recovery, body measurements) so you can view trends and so APEX Intelligence can reason about patterns across time;

- Maintain and improve the Services, including diagnosing bugs and understanding feature usage at an aggregate level;

- Communicate with you about your account or the Services; and

- Comply with legal obligations and protect the security and integrity of the Services.

We do not use your health or wellness data to serve third-party advertising, and we do not sell your personal data.

# 5. How We Share Personal Data

We share personal data only as described below:

- AI service providers: Anthropic (Claude) processes data you submit for AI-powered features (meal photo analysis, coaching responses, weekly review generation). See Section 8 for details.

- Infrastructure providers: our backend (Supabase) stores account and application data on our behalf, under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

- Legal and safety: we may disclose data where required by law, to enforce our Terms of Use, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of APEX, our users, or others.

- Business transfers: if APEX is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy or a successor policy.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes, and we do not sell personal data.

# 6. Your Choices

**Access and correction. **You can view and edit most of your profile and logged data directly within the app (Settings, body measurement entries, meal logs).

**Deletion. **You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below. We may retain limited data where required by law or for legitimate record-keeping (e.g. fraud prevention), and we may retain aggregated or de-identified data that no longer identifies you.

**Redo Program. **You can change your training goal, training days, and equipment access at any time via Settings → Redo Program. This does not delete or alter any of your historical logged data — it only changes your active training plan going forward.

**AI coaching data. **You can request deletion of your APEX Intelligence conversation history by contacting us; this does not delete your underlying logged data (meals, recovery, measurements), which is retained separately as described in Section 3.

# 7. Data Security and Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your personal data, including encryption in transit and access controls on our backend infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Logged historical data (meals, recovery, body measurements) is retained indefinitely while your account is active, since it is the basis for trend tracking and AI Intelligence pattern recognition — you may request deletion at any time as described in Section 6.

# 8. AI Features and Third-Party AI Technology

**How APEX uses AI. **APEX uses Anthropic’s Claude AI models to power several features, including:

- Estimating food items, portions (in grams), and macros from meal photos you submit;

- Generating and adjusting your training plan based on your goal, training days, and equipment access;

- APEX Intelligence (Engine B) coaching responses, including weekly reviews that combine your APEX Shield score with your logged adherence and history.

**What data is shared with Anthropic. **Data you submit for AI processing (such as meal photos or coaching questions) is sent to Anthropic’s API to generate a response. We do not control Anthropic’s infrastructure directly; Anthropic processes this data under its own API terms and does not use API data to train its models by default.

**Limitations of AI Technology. **AI-generated output, including meal macro estimates, training plans, and coaching responses, may contain errors and should not be treated as guaranteed accurate. Portion and macro estimates from photos are approximations — you can always review and adjust any estimated item before or after it is saved. AI coaching output is not medical advice and is not a substitute for consultation with a qualified healthcare professional, particularly regarding any health condition, injury, pregnancy, or significant change to diet or exercise routine.

# 9. Children's Data

APEX is not directed to and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected personal data from a person under 18, we will delete it as quickly as reasonably possible. If you believe a minor has provided us personal data, please contact us at the email below.

# 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as APEX evolves and grows. We will post the updated Policy with a new “Last Updated” date, and where changes are material, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users directly (e.g. via in-app notice or email).
